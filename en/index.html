How to use the Application

How to use the Application

1
Download the app
  • Download the app from App Store or Google Play.
App Store Google Play
  • Make sure your device supports the application
2
Complete registration
  • Open the app and start the registration process.
  • Enter your details: name, email and phone number.
3
Submit driver's license
  • Upload a photo or scan of your driver's license
  • Wait for document verification.
4
Choosing a scooter
  • Open the map in the app.
  • Find the nearest available scooter.
  • Check scooter information (fuel amount, distance).
5
Booking or rental
  • Click on the selected scooter on the map.
  • Select "Book" or "Rent" option.
  • If booking, you'll have 10-15 minutes to reach the scooter.
6
Scan QR code
  • Approach the scooter and find the QR code on the handlebar.
  • Scan the QR code through the app.
  • Wait for unlock confirmation.
7
Start your ride
  • Make sure the scooter is ready to ride.
  • Click "Start Ride" button in the app.
  • Enjoy your ride!
8
End your ride
  • Find an authorized parking spot on the map.
  • Park the scooter in the designated area.
  • Click "End Ride" button in the app.
9
Photo report
  • Take a photo of the scooter after the ride.
  • Make sure the photo is clear and shows the scooter's condition.
  • Confirm the photo report in the app.
