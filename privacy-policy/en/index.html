Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [28.04.2025]

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Lavanta Ride (hereinafter referred to as the "Application," "we," "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our scooter rental services in accordance with applicable Turkish legislation, including the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (KVKK).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: name, phone number, email address, driver's license details (if necessary), and photos (if uploaded).
  • Payment Information: bank card or payment system details used for scooter rentals, processed through a secure third-party provider (we do not store your card details).
  • Location Data: GPS data to display nearby scooters and track trips.
  • Usage Information: trip history, rental duration, routes taken, and user preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide scooter rental services and calculate trip costs.
  • To ensure the security of the Application and payment processes.
  • To display nearby available scooters and optimize routes.
  • To communicate with you (booking confirmations, notifications, support).
  • To conduct analytics and improve service quality and user experience.
  • To fulfill our legal and regulatory obligations under KVKK.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following cases:

  • With payment providers to process transactions (we do not store your card details).
  • With contractors and technical partners assisting in the operation of the Application.
  • With government authorities if required by law or regulations.
  • In case of business transfers, such as mergers or acquisitions.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill legal obligations. We implement technical and organizational security measures, including encryption and access control, in accordance with KVKK requirements.

5. Your Rights (Under KVKK)

You have the right to:

  • Know whether your personal data is being processed.
  • Request information about the processing of your data.
  • Obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate or unlawfully processed data.
  • Withdraw your consent if processing is based on your consent.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: info@lavantaride.com

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All changes will be available in the Application along with the updated effective date. Continued use of the Application after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new terms.
